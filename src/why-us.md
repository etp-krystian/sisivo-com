## Best-fit client profile

- B2B SaaS, tech-enabled, or complex-service teams with 20-80 employees
- 1-5 people in sales, often founder-led or led by a Head of Sales
- teams already running outbound and using HubSpot, Salesforce, or a similar CRM
- companies under pressure to make pipeline more predictable without hiring heavily

## What to expect in the first phase

<ul class="step-flow">
  <li><strong>Week 1:</strong> pipeline diagnosis, CSV review, and stage-quality analysis.</li>
