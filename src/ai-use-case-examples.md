If your team is asking "what should we automate first?", these are common starting points.

Each example is designed to be understandable for non-technical teams and measurable from a business perspective.

## How to choose your first use case

<ul class="step-flow">
  <li><strong>Pick frequent work:</strong> Choose tasks your team repeats every week.</li>
  <li><strong>Define clear metrics:</strong> Measure time saved, cycle-time reduction, quality, or revenue impact.</li>
  <li><strong>Start with one workflow:</strong> Keep the first scope focused, then scale based on results.</li>
</ul>
