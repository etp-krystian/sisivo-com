If you are wondering whether Sisivo fits your team, start with one active pipeline.

Do not begin with a broad transformation project or a full CRM rebuild.

## Best starting conditions

- an active B2B pipeline with recent deal activity
- a founder or Head of Sales who owns the process
- a CRM export that shows stages, deal values, and recent activity

## Simple first-step plan

<ul class="step-flow">
  <li><strong>1. Export one recent pipeline:</strong> usually the last 3-6 weeks from HubSpot, Salesforce, or a similar CRM.</li>
  <li><strong>2. Review the actual deal flow:</strong> identify dead deals, weak stages, poor qualification, and lost urgency.</li>
  <li><strong>3. Run a focused pilot:</strong> apply simple rules for ICP, stage quality, next steps, and deal decisions.</li>
  <li><strong>4. Decide based on evidence:</strong> expand only after the pilot shows better control, better conversion, or cleaner forecast quality.</li>
</ul>
