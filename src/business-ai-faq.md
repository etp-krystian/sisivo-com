## Is this only for large companies?
No. Most projects start with one workflow and one measurable goal.

## Do we need a full data cleanup first?
No. We define the minimum data needed for the first useful implementation.

## How fast can we launch?
First implementations are often delivered in 4-8 weeks.

## Will this replace our team?
No. The goal is to remove repetitive admin work and support better decisions.

## How do we measure success?
We define measurable metrics up front: response speed, qualification quality, manual time saved, and workflow reliability.
