This FAQ is for business teams that want practical AI decisions without technical jargon.

## Is AI only for large enterprises?

No. Smaller and mid-size companies often benefit faster because they can implement focused changes quickly.

## Do we need an internal AI team first?

No. You need process knowledge, decision ownership, and clear goals. Technical delivery can be supported externally.

## How long does a first project usually take?

A focused pilot is often delivered in 4 to 8 weeks, depending on scope and integration complexity.

## How should we think about budget?

Start with one measurable use case. This lowers risk and lets you invest based on proven outcomes.

## What if our data is incomplete or messy?

That is common. We design around current reality and improve data quality as part of implementation.

## Will AI replace our team?

In most business contexts, AI removes repetitive tasks and supports better decisions. People stay in control of critical actions.

## How do we define success?

Before implementation, we agree on metrics such as time saved, response time, conversion improvement, error reduction, or throughput.

## What is the best first step if we are unsure?

Book a short intro call and describe your workflow in simple terms. We will recommend a practical starting point.
