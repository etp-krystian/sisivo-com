## Is Sisivo a CRM replacement?
No. Sisivo works with your current CRM. The CRM stores data. Sisivo adds the operating logic: who you should target, how deals are qualified, how stages are defined, and what next action makes sense.

## What do we need to start?
The minimum starting point is simple: an active pipeline, a clear owner on the client side, and a recent CSV export from your CRM. We do not need a perfect setup or a full data cleanup to begin.

## How quickly do teams see value?
The first useful signal usually appears in 1-2 weeks. That often means spotting dead deals, stage problems, and qualification gaps early. A practical first rollout usually takes 2-4 weeks.

## What if we already have HubSpot or Salesforce?
That is normal. Sisivo is designed for teams that already use a CRM but still lack a repeatable sales system. The point is not to replace the stack. The point is to make the stack operationally useful.

## Is this consulting or software?
It starts like an operating system with implementation support. The value comes from getting a working model into the team quickly, not from slide decks or broad advisory work.

## When is Sisivo not a fit?
It is usually not a fit for teams without an active pipeline, companies that only want more leads, or businesses that are too early to commit to a sales owner and basic process changes.
